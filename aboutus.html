LVinnamon

History

Our CEO and Founder Shanon Mekarsari started a home bakery on 20 June 2019 on a whim as a result of the pandemic. Shanon has always been dreaming of opening a bakery since her teenage years and when the pandemic hit her dream became stronger than ever and now we have more than 100 branches all over Indonesia.

Our Vision and Mission

At LVinnamon, our mission is to be the global leader in providing exceptional cinnamon rolls and baked goods. We are committed to using premium ingredients, delivering unmatched taste and freshness. With a focus on customer satisfaction and sustainable practices, we aim to create memorable experiences while making a positive impact in our communities.

Logo Background

Our logo has the meaning of a humble start. The house in the middle of the logo is a symbol of a home bakery with a hint of warmness in it indicating that it is first a home bakery and because of the overwhelming love and support from our customers, it has come to the point that it is in right now.
